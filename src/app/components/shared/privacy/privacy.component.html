We UBoxIT (www.uboxit.nl and apps), registered as Your Delicacy B.V in Netherlands. UBoxIT will comply with rules and regulations of data protection. We use data and information you provide while using services through website and mobile apps which are personalization for customers.

What we collect?

  • UBoxIT collects below personal details which you provide as part of use of our services
    • first and last name
    • email address
    • mobile number
    • delivery address
    • residence address
    • location data
    • date of birth
    • any other information voluntarily provided
    • IP – address
    • Internet browser (cookies) and device type
    • Previous order and selection
  • Above personal information is collected during
    • Order food on the website/ mobile apps
    • Create profile or sign up to have login
    • Subscribing to the offer and promos
    • Providing feedback or ratings

What we do with collected data?

  • UBoxIT uses the personal information collected to improve the content of the website/app, personalize the offer based on customer preference and data, communicate information to the customer, and for any other specified purposes.
  • We use the data to create personal account with UBoxIT.
  • We use the data for any communication to inform the order status.
  • We use the data to refund incase the order doesn’t get delivered or not meet customer needs.
  • We use the data to provide better details or order status when you reach us through our customer service to make faster resolution.
  • During order processing on our site or mobile app, we use your email and mobile details to send the confirmation through third party.
  • We use your email to distribute the campaign and offers, you can unsubscribe at any point.
  • Online ordering system stores information about the past orders in order to enable quick reordering in the future or revisit.

Correction of personal data

UBoxIT ensures the data collected are accurate and up to date.

As a customer, you are allowed to change the personal details or profile at any moment through online channels website or mobile apps after login. Otherwise, contact our privacy policy team through privacypolicies@uboxit.nl

Customer may also ask to delete the profile and personal information by contacting privacypolicies@uboxit.nl
